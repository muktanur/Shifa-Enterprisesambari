How Shifa Store is Revolutionizing 10-Minute Delivery in Goalpara

Introduction: The Rise of Instant Delivery

In today's fast-paced world, waiting 30–45 minutes for groceries feels like an eternity. The quick-commerce revolution — driven by companies like Blinkit and Zepto in metros — has fundamentally changed what consumers expect. People no longer plan grocery runs; they simply reach for their phones and expect items at their doorstep in minutes.

Enter Shifa Store — Assam's answer to the quick-commerce wave, right here in Ambari. We're bringing big-city delivery speeds to our local community, powered by a dedicated team of riders and a tech-first approach.

"We believe that fast, affordable delivery isn't just for metro cities. Every family in Ambari deserves the convenience of having groceries delivered in minutes." — Shifa Enterprisesambari Team

Benefits of 10-Minute Delivery

Why does delivery speed matter so much? Here's what our customers told us:

  • No more last-minute stress — Forgot an ingredient mid-cooking? We've got you.
  • Save time for what matters — Spend time with family, not at the grocery store.
  • Reduce impulse buying — Order only what you need, when you need it.
  • Fresher products — Shorter time from shelf to door means fresher goods.
  • Emergency access — Baby essentials, medicines, or snacks — available any time.

How Shifa Store Makes It Happen

The secret behind our 10-minute promise lies in our hyper-local fulfilment model. We operate micro-warehouses — called dark stores — within 1–2 km of high-density neighbourhoods. This eliminates long transit distances and allows our riders to reach any address in the delivery zone in under 10 minutes.

Our in-house logistics software optimises rider routes in real time, ensuring the fastest path from our store to your home. Every order is picked, packed, and dispatched in under 2 minutes, giving riders 8 minutes to cover the remaining distance.

Customer Convenience & Reliability

Reliability isn't just about speed. It's about trust. At Shifa Store, we back every order with our On-Time Guarantee: if your delivery exceeds 15 minutes, you receive a discount on your next order. Our customer satisfaction rate stands at 97%, and we're proud of every single order we deliver.

From real-time GPS tracking to 24/7 customer support, every touchpoint is designed to make you feel confident and taken care of.

The Future of Quick Commerce in Assam

We're just getting started. Shifa Store plans to expand its dark store network across Goalpara district, introduce same-day fresh produce subscriptions, and launch a loyalty programme that rewards our most frequent customers. Our vision is to be the most trusted delivery partner for every household in Assam.

Quick commerce isn't a trend — it's the new normal. And Shifa Store is here to lead that change in Northeast India, one 10-minute delivery at a time.
